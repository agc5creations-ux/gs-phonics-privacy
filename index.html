PRIVACY POLICY

G's Phonics: Learn to Read

Effective: June 14, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 14, 2026

The short version. A parent signs up with an email. Kids get a nickname and a cartoon avatar — no real names, photos, location, or birthdays. When a child sounds out a letter, the audio clip is sent to OpenAI's Whisper service only to check the answer and is deleted immediately after. We don't show ads, sell data, or use trackers.

1. What we collect

From parents

  • Email address — to sign in and (if requested) for account recovery.
  • Password — stored only as a one-way bcrypt hash.

From kid profiles

  • First name or nickname (chosen by parent)
  • Cartoon avatar choice
  • Difficulty level (Easy / Medium / Hard)
  • Game progress (levels completed, stars earned)

Microphone

The microphone activates only while the child holds the in-app mic button. The audio is sent over HTTPS to our server, forwarded once to OpenAI Whisper for transcription, then the audio bytes are deleted within ~1–2 seconds. We never store, replay, or share recordings.

We do not collect

Real names, addresses, birthdays, phone numbers, photos, contacts, location, advertising identifiers, social handles, payment data, or biometrics.

2. How we use information

  • Let the parent sign in and switch between kid profiles.
  • Save level progress and stars between sessions.
  • Verify pronunciation during the "Say it!" game.
  • Investigate abuse or security incidents.

We do not use data for advertising, profiling, or ML training.

4. Children's privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

  • The app is intended for ages 3–7 and operated by a parent or guardian.
  • We collect only what's needed to deliver reading lessons.
  • The app contains no chat, no user-to-user communication, no ads, no IAP.
  • Parents can review or delete kid data from the in-app Parent area (gated by a math question).

5. Your choices & rights

  • Edit a kid's name, avatar, or difficulty in the Parent area.
  • Delete a kid profile — wipes their progress immediately.
  • Delete the parent account — email us; we'll complete the deletion within 30 days.
  • Export your data — email us for a JSON copy.
  • Withdraw consent for microphone — simply don't tap the mic; Tap It and Trace It games work without audio.

EU / UK / California residents have additional rights under GDPR, UK-GDPR, and the CCPA, including the right to lodge a complaint with your data-protection authority.

6. Retention

  • Active accounts: kept while the account exists.
  • Deleted accounts: purged within 30 days; backups expire within 90 days.
  • Server logs: rotated and deleted within 30 days.
  • Audio recordings: never retained beyond a single transcription request.

7. Security

  • All traffic is encrypted with TLS / HTTPS.
  • Passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes only.
  • Production database access is restricted to the developer.
  • We will notify affected parents within 72 hours of any confirmed data breach.

8. International transfers

Some processing may happen on servers outside your country (e.g. OpenAI's US endpoints). When that happens we rely on standard contractual safeguards.

9. Changes

If we make material changes we'll update the "Last updated" date and email registered parents about significant ones before they take effect.
